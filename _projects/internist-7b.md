A 7B-parameter medical large language model and the first model of its size to surpass **60% on MedQA**.
Trained on a curated mix of high-quality medical and general-domain data, Internist-7B was designed
to study how data composition and scale shape clinical reasoning in compact models. It serves as the
research foundation for the clinical agent now deployed at the Cliniques Universitaires Saint-Luc.
