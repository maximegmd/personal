An internal LLM-serving and orchestration stack powering the clinical agent deployed inside the Epic
electronic health record at the Cliniques Universitaires Saint-Luc. Chopper handles model serving,
inference routing, safety guardrails, auditability, and monitoring for an agentic system used by
**3000+ clinicians** across **150,000+ clinical interactions**.
